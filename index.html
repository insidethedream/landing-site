How We Help

We understand that graduation is a very important part of any students career and we want them to make the most of it. Inside the Dream provides students with free formal attire. We are able to provide our services through generous donations of new and gently used formal dresses, gowns, suits, tuxedos, as well accessories and shoes. These items are given to students at no cost and remain theirs to keep.

Every year Inside the Dream helps up to 300 students at our Boutique Day, with a staff of approximately 100 dedicated volunteers. Without the commitment of our volunteers, donors, and gracious sponsors, none of this would be possible.

With your help we can continue to provide this service to our community. By supporting Inside the Dream, you'll be giving an underprivileged student an unforgettable experience and helping them celebrate their graduation with their peers.

Our Mission

Ruth Renwick, Co-Founder

Our mission has always been to help underprivileged students attend their prom and graduation celebrations with style and glamour by providing free formal-wear. Inside the Dream is run entirely by volunteers and relies solely on donations.

We have been proudly serving the community since 2003.

Mary Kay is proud to be a small part of the wonderful Inside the Dream program and I hope that in some way, we have helped make these inspiring students' graduation night celebration a memorable one."
